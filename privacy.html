Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 4, 2025

1. Information Collection

CNC Workpiece Calculator does not collect or store any personal information, usage data, or device identifiers. The app runs entirely offline and never transmits your inputs to external services.

2. Use of Information

Because no data is collected, no data is used, processed, shared, or sold to any third parties.

3. Data Security

All geometry, tolerance, and weight calculations occur on your device. User inputs remain in local memory only for the duration of the session and are not persisted or synced.

4. Changes to This Policy

If the app’s functionality changes in a way that affects privacy, this page will be updated with the new policy and an effective date.

5. Contact

For privacy questions or support, email wangning.engineer@outlook.com. Typical response time is 24–48 hours.
