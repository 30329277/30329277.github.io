BeamDrop Support

BeamDrop lets you transfer photos from your iPhone or iPad directly to any computer on the same local network — no cables, no cloud, no account.

Getting Started

  • Connect your iPhone and computer to the same Wi-Fi network, or enable Personal Hotspot.
  • Open BeamDrop and select the photos you want to share.
  • On your computer's browser, type the address shown in the app (for example, http://172.20.10.1:8080).
  • Keep BeamDrop open and your screen on during the transfer.
