How It Works

  1. Browse the Schedule — Pick your team or browse by group/knockout phase.
  2. Add Your Matches — Tap a match to add it with your gate, section, and seat info.
  3. Get Your Timeline — Each match generates a personalized matchday timeline.
  4. Receive Reminders — Notifications alert you 1 day and 2 hours before kickoff.
  5. Use Offline Tools — Stadium maps, emergency info, currency, and phrases work without internet.

Works Completely Offline

Every feature works without an internet connection. No account required. No ads. No data collection. Perfect for traveling abroad where connectivity can be unreliable.

FAQ

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. All schedules, stadium data, maps, and tools work fully offline. Location features use on-device GPS only.

Does the app collect any personal data?

No. Zero data collection. See our Privacy Policy.

What about location data?

Location is processed on-device only to show your position on the map. It is never recorded or transmitted anywhere.

Are match times in my local timezone?

Yes. All kickoff times are stored as absolute timestamps and displayed in your device's current timezone automatically.

Can I turn off notifications?

Yes. You can deny notification permission when prompted, or disable it later in iOS Settings → WC26 Travel Companion → Notifications.

How do I contact support?

Email wangning.engineer@outlook.com. Typical response time is 24–48 hours.
