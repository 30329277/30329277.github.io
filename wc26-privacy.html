Privacy Policy — WC26 Travel Companion

Last updated: May 25, 2026

1. Information Collection

WC26 Travel Companion does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, analytics, or device identifiers to external servers. All match data and preferences are stored locally on your device using on-device storage (SwiftData).

2. Location Data

The app requests access to your device's location solely to show your position relative to nearby stadiums on the in-app map. Location data is processed entirely on your device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or sent to any server. You can deny or revoke location permission at any time in iOS Settings; the app remains fully functional without it.

3. Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications to remind you of upcoming matches. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data, tokens, or identifiers are sent to any external service.

4. Use of Information

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, no data is used, processed, shared, or sold to any third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising frameworks, or analytics services.

6. Data Security

All match schedules, stadium information, and user-created matchday data are stored locally on your device. No user inputs are synced to any cloud service.

7. Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from any user, including children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

If the app's functionality changes in a way that affects privacy, this page will be updated with the new policy and an effective date.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or support, email wangning.engineer@outlook.com. Typical response time is 24–48 hours.
